Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I get started with SmartBook?

You can be up and running in less than 30 minutes! Our simple onboarding process guides you through setting up your rooms, equipment, and user accounts.

Do you offer custom integrations?

Yes! We offer custom integrations with popular calendar systems, communication tools, and enterprise software. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 email support, live chat during business hours, and dedicated account managers for enterprise customers. Plus, we have an extensive knowledge base and video tutorials.

Is my data secure with SmartBook?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption, are SOC 2 compliant, and follow all major data protection regulations including GDPR. Your data security is our top priority.
